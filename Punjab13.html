Chatti di Lassi

Chatti di Lassi, or traditional Punjabi lassi served in earthenware pots, is a refreshing and nourishing beverage enjoyed by people of all ages across Punjab. Made from creamy yogurt, sugar, and flavored with cardamom or rose water, lassi is a cooling antidote to the sweltering heat of summer. The use of earthenware pots, known as chatti, to serve lassi is not just a matter of tradition but also adds a unique earthy flavor to the drink. The porous nature of the clay helps keep the lassi cool and enhances its taste, making it even more delightful to savor. Chatti di Lassi is not only a delicious thirst-quencher but also offers numerous health benefits. Rich in probiotics and nutrients, lassi aids in digestion, boosts immunity, and provides a natural source of hydration, making it a popular choice during hot summer days.
